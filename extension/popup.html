Export YouTube Cookies
How it works
YouTube blocks downloads from cloud servers by flagging their IPs as bots.
  • Export cookies from your browser where you're logged into YouTube
  • Upload the file to NexLoad via Settings → YouTube Cookies
  • Cookies last ~30 days before needing refresh
Why is this needed?
  • YouTube uses BotGuard to detect automated requests
  • Cloud IPs (Render, AWS, Vercel) are automatically flagged
  • Cookies prove the request comes from a real browser session
